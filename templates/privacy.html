Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name and email address. As you use Spendly, we store the expense records you add — including amounts, categories, dates, and any descriptions you provide.

We do not collect payment information, location data, or any data beyond what is necessary to operate the service.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide and improve the Spendly service. This includes displaying your expense history, generating summaries, and maintaining your account.

We do not sell your data, use it for advertising, or share it with third parties for any commercial purpose.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely and is accessible only to you via your account. We take reasonable technical measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, loss, or disclosure.

You may delete your account at any time. Upon deletion, all your personal data and expense records will be permanently removed from our systems.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly uses Google Fonts to load typefaces. This request is made directly from your browser to Google's servers and is subject to Google's own privacy policy. No other third-party services are used that have access to your personal data.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us. We are committed to addressing any privacy-related inquiries promptly and transparently.
